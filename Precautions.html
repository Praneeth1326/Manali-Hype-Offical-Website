Precautions

Item Details
Kettle Bring one kettle for warm water & water bottles.
Food Buy maggi packets (min large 5 packets) for cooking.
Power Banks Carry the power banks and if possible light equipment for night time purpose.
Clothing Bring at least one thermal dress, jackets, blankets, head caps, gloves for basic needs.
Footwear Bring 3 pairs of socks, 2 pairs of shoes, 3 or more pairs of t-shirts/hoodies/sweatshirts/cargos/jeans.
Personal Care Carry your own sunscreens/moisturizer/Vaseline/paste/brush/soap/oil etc.
Medical Carry the medical things for emergencies.
Camera Carry the camera for taking pictures (if possible).
Fun Activities Carry any fun activities things (like cards etc.).
